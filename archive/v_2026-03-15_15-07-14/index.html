Before you battle, check out the ELEMENT ROSTER — tap any element to see their full stats, abilities, and how each stat affects their combat style.

On the battle screen, your HP & mana are always visible at the top, and your target's health floats above them on the map.

HOW TO PLAY
🎯 Objective
First team to reach the kill limit wins. Default is 5 kills in a 3:30 match — both adjustable in Match Settings before the game. If time expires, the team with the most kills wins. Ties go to sudden death.
🎮 Controls
⌨ KEYBOARD / MOUSE
WASD — Move
Left Click — Auto-attack
Q / E / R — Ability 1 / 2 / Ultimate
F — Special
G — Rock Buster (fire at nearest obstacle)
Shift — Sprint
Tab — Cycle Target
U — Hold to view Scoreboard
Esc — Pause
💡 All bindings are remappable in Options → Controls. Tab (cycle target), U (scoreboard), and Select (scoreboard) are fixed.
⚡ Abilities & Mana
Each element has 3 abilities plus a special — all shown in the HUD at the bottom of the screen. Abilities cost mana, which regenerates passively. The special is free and recharges on its own cooldown.
Landing an ability opens a 0.5s window where your next auto-attack deals +20% damage. Hitting a CC'd target (stunned, rooted, frozen) deals +30% bonus damage.
💥 Special Ability — F
Your special is tied to your combat class, not your element. It costs no mana and recharges quickly.
⚔️ MELEE — SLAM (6s)
Ground-pound AOE around you. Stuns nearby enemies and damages obstacles in range. Heavier elements hit harder and wider.
🌀 HYBRID — SURGE (8s)
Dash forward and hit the first enemy or obstacle in your path. Slows what it hits and shoves obstacles aside.
🎯 RANGED — FOCUS (10s)
Fire a fast long-range skillshot beyond your normal attack range. Great for finishing fleeing targets or clearing obstacles from distance.
🌀 Warp Gates
Moving gate openings appear on each arena edge. Walk through one to teleport to the opposite side. Gates narrow from 3 per edge down to 1 as the match progresses. After warping, a 4.5s cooldown bar appears at the bottom of the screen. Hitting a closed section of wall bounces you back.
🌩️ Weather Zones
Weather zones drift across the map and affect all players equally — no elemental favorites. They turn neutral ground into contested space worth fighting over. If two zones overlap, both effects stack — the buffs shown under your character update in real time to reflect everything you're standing in.
🔥 HEATWAVE
Everyone deals +40% damage. High risk, high reward.
❄️ BLIZZARD
Everyone moves at −40% speed. A brutal slug-fest.
⚡ THUNDERSTORM
Cooldowns drain 55% faster for everyone inside. Ability spam zone.
🌧️ DOWNPOUR
Everyone heals 14 HP/s. Hard to finish kills inside.
🌪️ SANDSTORM
Ability range cut by 45%. Everything becomes a brawl.
🕳️ BLACK HOLE
Gravity pulls toward the center. Sprint to break free — walking won't save you.
🎁 Pickups
Health Packs (pink cross) heal 30% of your max HP over 3 seconds. Only usable below full health.
Mana Pots (blue bottle) restore 50% of your max mana instantly. Always usable — grab them aggressively to keep abilities cycling.
Both respawn on individual cooldowns after being picked up, and always spawn spread apart from each other.
🪨 Floating Obstacles
Obstacles drift, orbit, or bounce around the arena. They block projectiles, push characters on contact, and take damage from attacks. Large ones shatter into indestructible fragments when destroyed. Small ones just pop.
Use them: clear a lane before a fight, or body-slam them into enemies by sprinting into a collision. Heavier elements (FORGE, STONE) push obstacles harder.
💡 AOEs and black hole pulls pass through obstacles — regular projectiles don't.
🔥 Momentum & Passives
Momentum: Each kill grants a stack (max 2), adding +8% damage for 6 seconds. Two stacks triggers ON FIRE. All sources deal +15% bonus damage to targets below 20% HP.
Passives: Every element has a unique passive. See the Element Roster for details.
🔲 Shrinking Arena
The arena compresses to 40% of its original size by match end, forcing fights. Gate openings narrow from 3 per edge to 1 as the walls close in.
