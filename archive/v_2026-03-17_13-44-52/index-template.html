HOW TO PLAY
🎯 Objective
First team to reach the kill limit wins. Default is 5 kills in a 3:30 match — both adjustable in Match Settings. You can also set either to unlimited for a freeplay session. If time runs out with no winner, the match goes to sudden death.
👥 Couch Multiplayer
Up to two human players can play on the same screen, each with their own controller. In the hero select lobby, use the + / − buttons to add player slots and toggle each slot between Human and CPU.
In multiplayer, each player gets their own coloured identity — P1 in gold, P2 in cyan — visible on your character, your cursor during hero select, and your controller overlay. Either player can pause with their Start button. The camera zooms out to keep both players on screen.
💡 Set your own slot to CPU to watch a bot match in spectator mode. Press Tab / L3 to cycle between characters.

⚡ Abilities & Mana
Each element has 3 abilities plus a special — shown in the HUD at the bottom of the screen. Abilities cost mana, which regenerates passively. The special is free and has its own cooldown.
Landing an ability opens a 0.5s window where your next auto-attack deals +20% damage. Hitting a CC'd target deals +30% bonus damage. Each kill grants a momentum stack — two stacks in quick succession triggers ON FIRE for bonus damage.
When your HP drops below 20%, a red arrow appears around your character pointing toward the nearest health pack. A blue arrow does the same for mana packs — both pulse faster the more critical your situation.
Ultimates are powerful but can't one-shot a full health target — a single ult hit is capped at 85% of the target's max HP. Against already-damaged targets they hit full. Finish the job.
💥 Special Ability
Your special is tied to your combat class, not your element. Free, fast recharge.
⚔️ MELEE — SLAM
AOE ground-pound that stuns nearby enemies and damages obstacles. Heavier elements hit harder and wider.
🌀 HYBRID — SURGE
Dash forward and hit the first enemy in your path. Also works as an escape dash when you need to break away.
🎯 RANGED — FOCUS
Fast long-range skillshot beyond your normal attack range. Great for finishing targets or clearing obstacles from a distance.
💨 Sprint
Sprint breaks Stun, Slow, and Silence on activation. It does not break Freeze. Running into enemies while sprinting deals chip damage and a knockback impulse — heavier elements push harder. Sprint also grants full immunity to Black Hole and Singularity pull while active.
🌀 Warp Gates
Gate openings move along each arena edge. Walk through one to teleport to the opposite side. Gates narrow from 3 per edge to 1 as the match progresses. After warping, a 4.5s cooldown bar appears at the bottom of the screen — hitting a closed wall during that time bounces you back.
🌩️ Weather Zones
Weather zones drift across the arena and affect all players equally. They turn neutral ground into contested space worth fighting over. Buffs appear under your character in real time showing exactly what you are getting — step out and they disappear instantly.
🔥 HEATWAVE
+40% damage for everyone inside. High risk, high reward.
❄️ BLIZZARD
−40% move speed for everyone. A brutal slug-fest.
⚡ THUNDERSTORM
Cooldowns drain 55% faster. Ability spam zone.
🌧️ DOWNPOUR
Everyone heals 10 HP/s. Hard to finish kills inside.
🌪️ SANDSTORM
Ability range cut by 45%. Everything becomes a brawl.
🕳️ BLACK HOLE
Gravity pulls toward the centre. Sprint to break free.
🌪️ Storm Convergence
When two storms drift close enough to deeply overlap, they merge into a single more powerful zone with a fresh timer. Every combination creates a unique effect that neither storm had alone.
Two storms of the same type merge into a MEGA version — same effect but amplified by 50%. MEGA zones have a thick pulsing outer ring so you can spot them immediately. Your buff label will show MEGA when you step inside.
⚡🔥 PLASMA STORM
Fire + Lightning. Abilities recharge fast, damage boosted. Every hit you take deals damage back to your attacker.
🌪️🔥 FIRESTORM
Fire + Wind. More damage and faster movement. Fight hard or get left behind.
❄️🔥 FLASHPOINT
Fire + Ice. The zone detonates every 4 seconds, stunning and damaging everyone inside. Between blasts, everyone heals.
⚡🌪️ SUPERCELL
Lightning + Wind. All projectiles move faster and travel much further. Ranged heroes become lethal from across the map.
❄️⚡ WHITEOUT
Lightning + Ice. Everyone is frozen every 3 seconds. While frozen you are immune to incoming damage.
❄️🌪️ ARCTIC GALE
Wind + Ice. Extreme speed for everyone — slow heroes keep pace with fast ones. Abilities come off cooldown visibly faster.
🌪️💧 DUST DEVIL
Wind + Downpour. Knockback tripled on all hits. Enemy HP bars are hidden. Every attack sends someone flying.
🔥💧 MAGMA SURGE
Fire + Downpour. The zone deals 15 HP/s but grants 40% armour. Hit hard and absorb almost anything else.
❄️💧 PERMAFROST
Ice + Downpour. Slower movement, but ability damage doubled. Commit to fighting here.
⚡💧 SEISMIC CHARGE
Lightning + Downpour. Melee hits chain to all nearby enemies. Packing together is dangerous here.
🌀⚡ SINGULARITY
Black Hole + anything. Extreme gravitational pull. Sprint barely helps — find a warp gate.
💡 THE MAELSTROM — if three storms overlap at the same time, they collapse into a massive zone: all damage doubled, kills reset cooldowns, all pickups pulled to the centre. Lasts 15 seconds then implodes dealing 30% max HP to everyone still inside. Get out or get a kill.
🎁 Pickups
Health Packs (pink cross) heal 30% of your max HP over 3 seconds. A directional arrow appears around your character pointing toward the nearest one when your HP is critical.
Mana Pots (blue bottle) restore 50% of your max mana instantly. A blue arrow appears when you are running low. Grab them aggressively to keep abilities cycling.
🪨 Floating Obstacles
Obstacles drift, orbit, or bounce around the arena. They block projectiles, push characters on contact, and take damage from attacks. Large ones shatter into indestructible fragments. Small ones pop.
Sprint into them to knock them into enemies. Use Rock Buster to clear a lane or deny cover. Heavier elements push obstacles further.
🔥 Hazard Zones
Some abilities leave persistent ground zones that continue to deal damage or effects after the initial hit.
🔥 Flame Patch — EMBER
Burns for 8 damage/s for 3 seconds on the ground where Inferno lands.
🌀 Whirlpool — TIDE
Persists 3 seconds after cast — keeps pulling and dealing 12 damage/s.
💥 Aftershock — STONE
Cracked ground from SLAM slows anyone who steps in it for 2 seconds.
🔥 Hero Passives
Every hero has a unique passive that creates a deeper mechanical interaction. Full details are on each hero's Roster page — here is a quick reference.
🔥 EMBER — Flame Patch

🌊 TIDE — Whirlpool
Whirlpool persists 3s after casting — keeps pulling.
❄️ FROST — Shatter
Bonus damage on slowed targets (+20%) and frozen (+30%).
🌿 FLORA — Root Tether
Heals 6 HP/s while a rooted enemy is within 180px.
🪨 STONE — Aftershock
SLAM leaves a slow zone for 3 seconds.
💨 GALE — Tailwind
Sprinting charges next ability — +30% speed and range.
🌀 VOID — Phantom Step
After warping, one incoming hit phases straight through you.
✨ MYST — Arcane Echo
Killing with an ability refunds 50% of its cooldown.
⚡ VOLT — Static Charge
Autos build stacks (max 3) — next ability consumes them for +8% damage each.
⚙️ FORGE — Molten Core
While Iron Will is active, melee collisions deal +50% damage.
🔲 Shrinking Arena
The arena compresses to 40% of its original size by match end, forcing fights. Gate openings narrow from 3 per edge to 1 as the walls close in — plan your escape routes early.
